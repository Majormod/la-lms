Overview

This course will redefine how positioning is taught. Through compelling storytelling, powerful strategy, and original frameworks, Dr. Nanda equips entrepreneurs, leaders, marketers, and sales professionals to carve an enduring space in the mind of their customers. It draws upon Indian and global brands and includes 4 original, proprietary frameworks.

Original Frameworks by Dr. Nanda
  • Positioning Prism™ – How to create a 360° brand position across markets, minds, and moments.
  • Mindspace Laddering™ – How customers rank offerings mentally - and how to climb their mental ladder.
  • The Anchor-Aspire Gap™ – A powerful model showing how to position between what customers know and what they hope for.
  • The Obvious Trap™ vs. The Only Path™ – A strategic decision framework for standing out in crowded markets.

General Information

Leadership Accelerator is a premier coaching and training company dedicated to empowering emerging and established leaders with the skills, mindset, and strategies needed to excel in today's dynamic business environment. We offer a range of programs for individuals and corporations.

Our programs are designed for a wide audience, including aspiring leaders, new managers, senior executives, and entire corporate teams looking to enhance their leadership capabilities and drive organizational growth.

We offer a comprehensive suite of services including one-on-one executive coaching, group training programs for individuals, customized corporate workshops, leadership offsites, and long-term consulting engagements.

Our approach is highly personalized, practical, and results-oriented. We combine proven leadership principles with modern, data-driven insights and focus on real-world application to ensure lasting behavioral change and measurable impact.

Programs for Individuals

We offer several individual programs, such as the 'Emerging Leaders Track' for new managers and the 'Executive Presence Masterclass' for senior leaders. Each is designed to target specific career stages and development goals.

We recommend scheduling a free consultation with one of our program advisors. They will discuss your career goals, current challenges, and help you select the program that best aligns with your needs.

Yes, our one-on-one coaching is fully customized. While our group programs have a set curriculum, they include personalized feedback, goal-setting, and practical exercises tailored to the participants' industries and roles.

Yes, upon successful completion of our signature programs, participants receive a certificate of completion from Leadership Accelerator, which can be shared on professional networks like LinkedIn.

We offer flexible learning formats to suit your needs. Most of our programs are available online via our learning platform, and we also conduct exclusive offline workshops and hybrid models.

Programs for Corporates

For corporates, we offer a range of solutions including team-based training modules, leadership pipeline development programs, executive team alignment sessions, and strategic offsites designed to foster collaboration and drive performance.

Our process begins with a thorough discovery phase where we analyze your organization's goals, culture, and challenges. Based on this, we co-create a fully customized curriculum and delivery plan.

Absolutely. We specialize in strategic consulting to help organizations build a sustainable leadership pipeline. We partner with you to design, implement, and measure a multi-year development roadmap.

Yes, we offer all of the above. Our services range from single-day workshops and immersive offsite experiences to ongoing strategic consulting to address your specific organizational needs.

You can request a proposal or consultation by filling out the 'Corporate Enquiry' form on our website or by contacting our corporate solutions team directly via email or phone.

Payment and Pricing

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, bank transfers, and secure online payment gateways. For corporate clients, we follow a standard invoicing process.

Yes, for our individual signature programs, we offer flexible installment plans to make learning more accessible. Please contact our admissions team to learn more about the available options.

We often provide early-bird discounts for our individual programs and special rates for group enrollments of three or more participants from the same organization. Check our program pages for current offers.

Access and Tools

Tag Mango is our trusted third-party platform for hosting course content, managing community interactions, and delivering live sessions. It provides a seamless and interactive learning experience for our participants.

Upon enrollment, you will receive an invitation to join our program on the Tag Mango app, which is available for download on both iOS and Android devices. This will serve as your primary learning portal.

The app provides access to video modules, downloadable resources, workbooks, live session links, a community forum for peer interaction, and direct messaging for support.

Yes, participants typically receive lifetime access to the core program materials, including video lessons and downloadable resources, allowing you to revisit key concepts whenever needed.

Support and Contact

For general inquiries, you can reach us via the contact form on our website or email us at support@leadershipaccelerator.com. Enrolled participants receive access to a dedicated support channel within our learning app.

Yes, we encourage prospective individual clients and corporate partners to schedule a free, no-obligation consultation to discuss their needs and learn more about how we can help them achieve their goals.

Our refund and cancellation policies vary by program. Generally, for individual programs, we offer a satisfaction guarantee within a specified period. For corporate engagements, terms are outlined in the service agreement.

Results and Testimonials

Participants can expect to see tangible improvements in areas such as strategic thinking, communication, team motivation, decision-making, and overall executive presence, leading to enhanced performance and career growth.

Yes, we have a dedicated 'Success Stories' section on our website featuring testimonials and case studies from both individual and corporate clients who have benefited from our programs.

We use a multi-faceted approach to measure success, including pre- and post-program assessments, 360-degree feedback, performance metric tracking (for corporate clients), and qualitative feedback on behavior change and confidence levels.

Yes, where possible, we tie our coaching outcomes to key business metrics such as team engagement scores, productivity levels, employee retention rates, and project success rates, providing a clear ROI.

Many participants report immediate shifts in their mindset and approach. Measurable behavioral changes are often observed within the first 30-60 days as new skills are applied and reinforced.

Absolutely. Our core philosophy is "learn today, apply tomorrow." Every module is built around practical frameworks, tools, and exercises that can be implemented immediately in your day-to-day work.

We have worked with leaders across a diverse range of industries, including tech, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and professional services. Our leadership principles are universally applicable.

Yes, we can provide relevant case studies upon request during a consultation. These examples showcase specific challenges, our tailored solutions, and the resulting positive impact on leadership and business outcomes.

Our curriculum is continuously updated to reflect the latest research and address current challenges like remote team management, digital transformation, and leading with empathy. We stay at the forefront of leadership trends to ensure our content is always relevant.

We help individuals and businesses master critical and essential skills to stay ahead in rapidly changing business dynamics.

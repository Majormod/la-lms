What is The Leadership Quotient Test™?
The Leadership Quotient Test™ is a proprietary assessment to measure the multidimensional essence of leadership. Unlike personality tests or competency checklists, this test evaluates your:

Strategic Clarity

Your ability to see the big picture and make bold decisions.

People Influence

How you inspire, align, and empower others.

Execution Drive

Your commitment to deliver results despite challenges.

Inner Resilience

The mindset, courage, and adaptability you bring under pressure.

Built upon decades of global leadership research and Leadership Excellence Framework™, this test decodes what makes leaders trusted, followed, and remembered.
